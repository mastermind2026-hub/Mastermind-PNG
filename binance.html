Binance P2P & Crypto Fundamentals

Educational guide on peer-to-peer digital currency operations, account verification, and safety practices in Papua New Guinea.

GUIDE 01

Understanding Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Trading

Peer-to-Peer trading allows users to exchange digital currencies directly with one another using local payment solutions. Binance acts as an escrow service to hold funds safely until both parties confirm payment receipt.

GUIDE 02

Identity Verification (KYC) Rules

Before trading, users must complete Know-Your-Customer (KYC) verification using a valid Papua New Guinea Passport, Driver's License, or National Identity Card (NID) to maintain platform security.

GUIDE 03

Essential Security Practices

  • Enable 2FA: Always activate Two-Factor Authentication via Google Authenticator.
  • Verify Proof of Payment: Never release crypto funds from escrow until you confirm the fiat payment has cleared in your local bank account.
