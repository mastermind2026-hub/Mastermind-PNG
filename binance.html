Getting Started with Binance Essentials

Navigating cryptocurrency exchanges requires a focus on security, proper verification, and strict risk control.

💡 Quick Tip for PNG Traders: Always complete Identity Verification (KYC) using a valid passport or government ID before funding your trading account.

Core Trading Fundamentals:

  • Account Security: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via Google Authenticator or hardware keys prior to depositing assets.
  • Spot vs. Derivatives: Focus on Spot trading to understand asset ownership before exploring margin or futures products.
  • Risk Management: Never allocate funds you cannot afford to hold through high market volatility. Utilize Stop-Loss orders on every active position.
