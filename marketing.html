The PNG Growth Formula

Welcome to the first guide for the MASTERMIND collective. Learn how to sell online without a big budget.

1. The Mobile-First Mindset

In PNG, most of your customers use smartphones. Ensure your content is vertical-friendly and fast to load.

2. Content That Converts

Stop just posting product photos. Start telling stories about how your product solves a problem for a local customer.

3. Directing Your Traffic

Always use a clear Call-to-Action. Direct your followers to your WhatsApp Business link so you can talk to them personally.
