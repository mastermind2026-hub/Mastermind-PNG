Digital Marketing Framework

Actionable strategies for local businesses, content creators, and social sellers operating within Papua New Guinea.

PILLAR 01

1. Social Media Audience Building

Facebook remains the dominant digital platform across PNG. Business growth requires moving from casual posting to targeted content scheduling, community engagement, and clear direct-message sales routes.

  • Content Focus: Share authentic customer testimonials and video product demonstrations.
  • Local Targeting: Tailor campaigns to regional urban hubs like Port Moresby, Lae, and Mt. Hagen.
PILLAR 02

2. Direct-to-Consumer Communication

In market economies with evolving payment gateways, WhatsApp and direct messaging serve as vital sales channels for instant buyer communication and order confirmation.

PILLAR 03

3. Brand Identity & Digital Professionalism

A consistent logo, clean website presence, and prompt customer support differentiate successful sellers from informal online marketplaces.
