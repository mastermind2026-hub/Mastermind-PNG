Free Digital Marketing Blueprints

Practical frameworks to build your brand authority, capture local market demand, and convert leads in Papua New Guinea.

GUIDE 01

The PNG Facebook Organic Blueprint

Facebook drives the majority of consumer attention across Papua New Guinea. Relying solely on paid ads isn't necessary if your organic presentation is strong.

Actionable Execution Steps:

  • Optimize Page Profile: Set a clear profile picture, list an active WhatsApp contact number, and clearly display your physical location or delivery coverage zones.
  • Group Community Engagement: Share informative, non-spam posts inside active PNG buy-and-sell groups rather than just dropping sales links.
  • High-Engagement Captions: End every post with a simple call-to-action (e.g., "Comment below or inbox us on WhatsApp for daily pricing").
GUIDE 02

WhatsApp Business Sales Automation

Converting social media traffic into real sales requires a structured messaging funnel. Free tools within WhatsApp Business can save you hours every week.

Actionable Execution Steps:

  • Catalog Setup: Upload your core physical products or service menu with fixed pricing in PNG Kina (PGK) and clear product descriptions.
  • Label Tracking: Organize customer chats using labels like "Awaiting Payment", "BSP Transfer Verified", and "Order Dispatched".
  • Quick Replies: Pre-set instant responses for your local bank account details and delivery pickup schedules to close sales instantly.
GUIDE 03

Low-Budget Visual Content Creation

You don't need professional camera gear or expensive editing tools to generate trust and drive sales online.

Actionable Execution Steps:

  • Lighting Focus: Always record product videos or client walkthroughs under indirect outdoor sunlight for high clarity.
  • Short-Form Video (Reels): Record 15-to-30-second clips showing product unboxings, real-time tests, or behind-the-scenes work.
  • Consistency: Publish at least 3 to 4 times per week during peak traffic hours (around 12:00 PM and 6:00 PM PNG time).
