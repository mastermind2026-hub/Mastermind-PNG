Community Earning Model

The MASTERMIND collective is built on a simple principle: Collaborate, Subscribe, and Share Revenue.

How it works:

  • Collaborate: Members share skills, strategies, and local market insights to identify new opportunities.
  • Subscribe: Active members gain access to exclusive content, tools, and shared resources.
  • Share Revenue: By working together, we aim to scale operations and distribute value back to the collective.

We are stronger together. Join us and start contributing to the growth of our shared economy in PNG.
