Physical Products & Reselling Guide

Practical sourcing strategies, profit calculation frameworks, and supplier verification practices for PNG entrepreneurs.

MODULE 01

1. Sourcing Fundamentals & Profit Margins

Before purchasing inventory, it is vital to calculate your true landed cost in PNG. A common mistake local sellers make is ignoring freight, customs duties, and local transport expenses.

The Landed Cost Formula:

Landed Cost = Supplier Item Price + International Freight + Duty Fees + Local Delivery

Key Sourcing Rule:

  • 3X Rule: Aim to sell physical items at 2.5x to 3x your wholesale purchase price to ensure healthy profit margins after accounting for shipping costs.
  • Weight Considerations: Focus on small, high-value, lightweight electronics, apparel, or accessories to keep air freight costs minimal.
MODULE 02

2. Supplier Verification & Shipping to PNG

Selecting reliable global suppliers ensures product quality and protects your capital from online scams.

Best Practices for Overseas Suppliers:

  • Always Request Samples: Order a single test unit via express air cargo before placing bulk orders of 50+ units.
  • Verify Supplier Longevity: Work exclusively with verified suppliers on platforms like Alibaba or Made-in-China who have at least 3+ years of trading history.
  • Freight Route Selection: Use express air freight (DHL/FedEx) for high-margin small goods and sea freight containers for bulk heavy shipments entering Port Moresby or Lae ports.
